About Me

Hello! My name is Lisa. I am a front-end web developer proficient in HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap and Javascript. I am additionally skilled in MySQL, MongoDB, Git, Node.js and Handlebars.js. I am involved in The Charlotte Junior Devs group in which I attend networking events each month.
While not coding you can find me working out, baking, reading, or watching movies. I do various forms of exercise such as calisthenics, yoga, and kick-boxing. I have a twelve year old cat named Honey who enjoys hanging out with me while I code. In my free time you can find me spending time with family and friends. I also love the outdoors and staying active.
